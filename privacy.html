Privacy Policy

KZ Color Cards • Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KZ Color Cards collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our online multiplayer card game.

1. Information We Collect

The app may collect and store limited information needed to run multiplayer gameplay, including:

  • player nickname entered in the app,
  • room code and room status,
  • gameplay data such as player seat, turn status, card count, card/game state, winner, and match progress,
  • chat messages sent inside a game room,
  • a random client ID generated by the app to identify the player in a room.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not intentionally collect real name, home address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, payment information, or sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information only to:

  • create and join multiplayer rooms,
  • synchronize gameplay between players,
  • show player nicknames, turn status, and room status,
  • send and display room chat messages,
  • maintain the online multiplayer session.

4. Third-Party Service

The app uses Supabase as the backend service for database and realtime multiplayer features. Supabase may process the limited gameplay data needed to provide these online features.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Gameplay data and chat messages are not shared with advertisers. Data may be processed by Supabase only for the purpose of running the online multiplayer service.

6. Chat Messages

Chat messages are visible to players inside the same game room. Do not share private or sensitive information in the room chat.

7. Data Retention

Room, gameplay, and chat data may be stored temporarily for multiplayer functionality. Old, unused, or inactive data may be deleted during maintenance.

8. Security

We use Supabase database and security features to help store and synchronize game data. However, no online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended as a general entertainment game. We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided sensitive personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes, such as adding login, ads, analytics, purchases, profile pictures, or other online features.
